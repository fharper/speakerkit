# Surviving the JavaScript package managers jungle

Choosing a JavaScript package manager today can feel like navigating a jungle with a blindfold and a map written in hieroglyphs. npm, pnpm, Bun, Yarn, and others promise speed, reliability, and… Sanity? Yet every project seems to have a different lockfile drama waiting to ruin your day.

In this talk, we’ll cut through the hype and confusion, compare install speeds, disk usage, support, and compatibility to give you practical rules for picking the right tool for your team and your projects. You’ll leave with a clear strategy to tame the infamous node_modules while keeping your workflow and your sanity intact.
