# The little technical writing survival guide for the AI era

The age of artificial intelligence definitely brings new challenges for people writing technical content. How can we use LLMs while still keeping our voices and ensuring high quality content for our audiences? With an average organic search traffic drop of 68% in all industries, how can you maximize visibility for your hard work? Are there any themes or writing types that will give us the highest impact with our readership? Is there any way to automate and ensure better results when using AI to help us generate technical content? How to stay mindful of our usage when outsourcing our thinking to machines? This talk, also known as the little technical writing survival guide for the AI era, will answer all these questions, and possibly more!
