# AI for senior developers: the wins, the risks, and the blind spots

By now you’ve seen enough talks about vibe coding and artificial intelligence while experimenting with it yourself. Some said it changed their lives. Others said it’s evil. One showed your tricks to be more efficient with it. Others demonstrated different tools out there to help you make up your mind.

But outside of the hype and usefulness of LLMs, have you ever stopped to really consider the implications for senior developers and leadership teams? Did you evaluate properly how it can really help you achieve software development marvels versus where the hype stops?

Most of us jumped on the bandwagon without a second thought! In this talk, we’ll have a realistic overview of the landscape right now, what it means for the future, how to implement bulletproof AI strategies, identify potential risks, and how to empower your team to be successful in this new AI era.
